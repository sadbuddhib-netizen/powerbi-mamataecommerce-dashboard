# powerbi-mamataecommerce-dashboard
📊 Mamata Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ecommerce sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and profit trends across categories and regions.

The dashboard enables business users to quickly monitor KPIs, identify high-performing segments, and make data-driven decisions.

🎯 Project Objective

To transform raw ecommerce sales data into meaningful insights that help understand:

overall sales performance

profit trends over time

customer buying patterns

category & payment preferences

regional sales distribution

📈 Key Business Insights

✔ Total Sales: 191K revenue generated
✔ Total Profit: 19K overall profit
✔ Total Quantity Sold: 1,944 units
✔ Average Order Value: 50K

✔ Clothing contributes the highest quantity share (58%)
✔ COD is the most preferred payment mode (44%)
✔ Maharashtra leads in total sales
✔ February recorded peak monthly profit
✔ Bookcases generate the highest sub-category profit
